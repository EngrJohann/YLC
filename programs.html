Toddler Program

Ages 2-3 Years

Our Toddler Program provides a gentle and nurturing introduction to preschool life. We focus on building confidence, developing social skills, and encouraging exploration through age-appropriate play and activities.

Sensory play activities
Early socialization skills
Motor skills development
Music and movement
Story time sessions
Creative art activities

Schedule & Details

Class Hours: 8:00 AM - 11:00 AM or 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Class Size: Maximum 12 students per class

Teacher Ratio: 1:6 (One teacher for every 6 students)

Nursery Program

Ages 3-4 Years

The Nursery Program builds on foundational skills with more structured learning activities. Children develop independence, enhance communication skills, and begin exploring early literacy and numeracy concepts.

Early literacy introduction
Basic numeracy skills
Creative expression through art
Cooperative play activities
Nature and science exploration
Physical education

Schedule & Details

Class Hours: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM or 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Class Size: Maximum 15 students per class

Teacher Ratio: 1:8 (One teacher for every 8 students)

Kinder Program

Ages 4-6 Years

Our comprehensive Kinder Program prepares children for elementary school success. With a focus on academic readiness, critical thinking, and social-emotional development, students build the skills and confidence needed for their educational journey.

Reading and writing skills
Math and problem-solving
Science experiments
Social studies introduction
Character development
School readiness preparation

Schedule & Details

Class Hours: 8:00 AM - 1:00 PM (Full Day Program)

Class Size: Maximum 18 students per class

Teacher Ratio: 1:10 (One teacher for every 10 students)
